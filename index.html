Pappus Chains

by Daniel Beckwith

Numberphile Video
Source

The number in each circle shows the ratio of that circle's radius to the radius of the outside circle. So if the circle has a 23 in it, that circle's radius is one 23rd of the outside circle's radius.
The circles are constructed using circle inversion, which is described well in the Numberphile video. Click the "Show Construction" button below the diagram to show the circles used to construct the chains and the circle used as the "mirror" (colored green).
